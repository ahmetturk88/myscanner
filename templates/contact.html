Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a vulnerability scan?
Simply navigate to the Vulnerability Scanner page, enter your target URL or IP address, select the scan type, and click "Start Vulnerability Scan". Results will appear within minutes.
Is my data secure with MyScanner?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmissions. Your scan results are stored securely and only accessible by you.
What is the difference between ZAP and OpenVAS?
ZAP specializes in web application scanning (XSS, SQL Injection). OpenVAS focuses on network infrastructure scanning (ports, services, CVEs). Both are available on our platform.
How can I get API access?
API access is available for Business plan customers. Please contact our sales team to get your API key and documentation.
Do you offer enterprise support?
Yes! We offer dedicated enterprise support with 24/7 response time, SLA guarantees, and custom integration assistance. Contact us for more details.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual subscriptions.
